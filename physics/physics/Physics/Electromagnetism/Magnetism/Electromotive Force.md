Emfs are any influence that causes charge to circulate around a closed path. and are present in [[Circuits|batteries]]. 

When dealing with [[Inductance|inductance]], emf is calculated by 
$$
\mathcal{E} = \frac{1}{q}\int f \cdot ds = - \frac{d \phi}{dt},
$$
where $f$ is the force the charges experience, and $\phi$ is [[Magnetic Field|magnetic flux]].
