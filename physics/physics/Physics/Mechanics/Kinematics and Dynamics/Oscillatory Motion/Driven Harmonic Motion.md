Driven Harmonic Motion can be written in the form
$$
\ddot{x} + 2\gamma\dot{x} + ax = C_0e^{i\omega t},
$$
whose solution is
$$
x(t) = \Bigl( \frac{C_0}{-\omega^2+2i\gamma\omega_0+a} \Bigr)e^{i\omega_0t},
$$
and the complete solution is
$$
x(t) = e^{-\gamma t}(Ae^{t\sqrt{\gamma^2-a}}+Be^{-t\sqrt{\gamma^2-a}})+\Bigl( \frac{C_0}{-\omega^2+2i\gamma\omega_0+a} \Bigr)e^{i\omega_0t}.
$$
