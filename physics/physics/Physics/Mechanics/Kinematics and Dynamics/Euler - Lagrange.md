Define a coordinate $q$ that describes the state of a system. Then define a kinetic [[Energy|energy]] $K$ and a potential energy $V$ such that 
$$K = K(\dot{q}),\, \, \, V = V(q).$$Since energy is conserved, 
$$
\frac{d}{dt}\frac{\delta K}{\delta\dot{q}} = -\frac{\delta V}{\delta q}.
$$
the LHS being the generalized momentum, and the RHS being the generalized force.
