The acceleration four vector when $\textbf{v} = v \hat{\textbf{x}}$ is
$$
a^\mu= (\gamma^4va_x,\gamma^4a_x,\gamma^2a_y,\gamma^2a_z).
$$
And to transform from $S'$, the momentary rest frame, back to $S$,
$$
\textbf{a} = \Bigl(\frac{a'_x}{\gamma^3},\frac{a'_y}{\gamma^2},\frac{a'_z}{\gamma^2}\Bigl).
$$
