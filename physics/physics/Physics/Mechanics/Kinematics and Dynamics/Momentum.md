Momentum is conserved within a closed system.

Momentum can be calculate as 
$$
\mathbfit{p}(t_2) - \mathbfit{p}(t_1) = \int_{t_1}^{t_2}\mathbfit{F} \, dt.
$$
The Center of Mass is defined as 
$$
\mathbfit{R}_{CM} \equiv \frac{\sum m_i r_i}{M}
$$
and remains still in a closed system.

In a 1-D elastic collision, the relative velocity of the two particles after the collision is the negative of the relative velocity before the collision.
$$
v_i-V_i = -(v_f-V_f).
$$
