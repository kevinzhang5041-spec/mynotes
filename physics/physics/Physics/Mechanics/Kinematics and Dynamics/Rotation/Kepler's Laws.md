1. The Trajectories of planets are conic sections, with a focus at the sun, bound orbits are ellipses. Unbound orbits are hyperbolas which contain parabolas as a special case.
2. The trajectories sweep out equal areas in equal times. (Look at orbit areas, compare to same semimajor orbits, ...)
3. When the orbit is bound, the period $T$ and semimajor axis $a$ obey $T^2 \propto a^3$ .
