Potential energy is defined as
$$
V(\mathbfit{r}) = - \int_{\mathbfit{r}_0}^{\mathbfit{r}} = \mathbfit{F}(\mathbfit{r}') \, \cdot d\mathbfit{r}',
$$
and can be rewritten as 
$$
\frac{1}{2}mv^2 + V(\mathbfit{r}) = E.
$$
Force is the gradient of the potential 
$$
\mathbfit{F}(\mathbfit{r}) = - \nabla U(\mathbfit{r}).
$$
Relates to [[Curl]].
