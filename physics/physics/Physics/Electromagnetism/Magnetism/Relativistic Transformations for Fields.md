The transformation for fields are
$$
\textbf{E}_{||}' = \textbf{E}_{||},
$$
$$
\textbf{B}_{||}' = \textbf{B}_{||},
$$
$$
\textbf{E}_{\perp}' = \gamma(\textbf{E}_{\perp} + \textbf{v} \times \textbf{B}_{\perp}),
$$$$
\textbf{B}_{\perp}' = \gamma(\textbf{B}_{\perp} - (\frac{\textbf{v}}{c^2}) \times \textbf{E}_{\perp}).
$$
Remember fields are smallest in the frame of the sources, and forces are largest in the frame of the particle.
