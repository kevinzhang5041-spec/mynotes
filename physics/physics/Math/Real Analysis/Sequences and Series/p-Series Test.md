A $p$-series has the form  
$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n^p}$$

- Converges if $p>1$
    
- Diverges if $p\le1$
    
- Important cases:
    
    - $p=1:\ \sum\frac1n$ (harmonic series) diverges
        
    - Larger $p$ $\Rightarrow$ faster decay
        
- Useful comparison benchmark for positive series
