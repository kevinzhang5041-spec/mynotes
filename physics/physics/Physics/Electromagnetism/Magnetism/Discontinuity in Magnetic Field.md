$$
B^+-B^-=\mu_0J_s.
$$Note that in a current carrying sheet, there is a stress ("pressure") of

$$
\frac{1}{2\mu_0}[(B^+)^2-(B^-)^2],
$$
where the field is the component parallel to the sheet. 

If we see a discontinuity in magnetic field, we can deduce there is a surface charge flowing, and that the surface is under stress.
