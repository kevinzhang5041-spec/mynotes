For AC currents, the complex quantities can be given by
$$
\tilde{V} = Z \tilde{I}
$$

where
$$
\tilde{V} = V_0e^{i\phi_V}
$$
$$
\tilde{I} = I_0e^{i\phi_I}
$$
$$
\tilde{Z} = |Z|e^{i\phi_Z}
$$
and impedance $\tilde{Z}$ can be calculated by
$$
R \rightarrow R
$$
$$
L \rightarrow i \omega L
$$
$$
C \rightarrow\frac{1}{i \omega C}
$$
From which we get
$$
V_0=I_0|Z|
$$
$$
\phi_V=\phi_I+\phi_Z
$$
These values are only defined for linear circuit elements.
Also
$$\overline{P_R} = \frac{V^2_{\text{rms}}}{R} = V_\text{rms}I_\text{rms}\cos(\phi)$$
