The angular momentum can be calculated by 
$$
\textbf{L} = \int \textbf{r} \, \times \, \textbf{v} \ dm
$$
$$
= \textbf{R} \, \times \, \textbf{P} + (I_z^{\text{CM}}\omega')\, \hat{\textbf{z}}.
$$
Can also be related using (for rigid bodies)
$$
I = \frac{L^2}{2E},
$$
and
$$
E = \frac{\omega L}{2},
$$
and
$$
T = \frac{\Sigma_{i, j} m_im_j(\textbf{r}_i-\textbf{r}_2)^2}{\Sigma_im_i}.
$$
