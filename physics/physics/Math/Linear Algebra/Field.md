Addition is commutative

$$  
x+y=y+x  
$$

Addition is associative

$$  
x+(y+z)=(x+y)+z  
$$

Additive identity

$$  
x+0=x  
$$

Additive inverse

$$  
x+(-x)=0  
$$

Multiplication is commutative

$$  
xy=yx  
$$

Multiplication is associative

$$  
x(yz)=(xy)z  
$$

Multiplicative identity

$$  
x\cdot 1=x,\qquad 1\neq 0  
$$

Multiplicative inverse

$$  
xx^{-1}=1,\qquad x\neq 0  
$$

Distributive law

$$  
x(y+z)=xy+xz  
$$
