Charges at rest are the source of a field $E$. Let frame $F'$ move with a velocity $\vec{v}$. The transformed field is
$$
E'_{||} = E_{||} 
$$
$$
E'_{\perp} = \gamma E_{\perp} 
$$
However, the force on a moving charge is independent on the charge's velocity. The force on such a charge is equal to $F=qE$.

See [[Relativistic Transformations for Fields]].
